This form describes a research study that is being conducted by the Collective Cognition Lab at the University of Rochester’s Department of Brain and Cognitive Sciences. The purpose of this study is to investigate mechanisms of decision-making under uncertainty.

If you decide to take part in this study, you will be asked to complete a study that will take about 90 minutes to complete. The task consists of 294 rounds of a simple decision-making task where you will be required to choose between two options presented on the screen by clicking on one. There will be short breaks throughout the task and at the end you will have two brief questions (max. 50 words response) to answer regarding your experience during the task. Please do not use AI generated responses. Detailed instructions for the task will be given once you begin.

You will be paid based on your score in the task (minimum of $15, up to $18). No direct payments will be sent from the University of Rochester research team.

You cannot do this study if you are color blind or have uncorrected visual impairment that precludes responding via an electronic screen.

We estimate that approximately 200 subjects will take part in this study.

We do not anticipate any risks or discomforts from your participation in this study. There are no expected benefits. There will be no cost to you to participate in this study.

This study will use the Prolific platform to collect your research data and, therefore, your user information may be used per Prolific’s privacy policy. The University of Rochester makes every effort to keep the information collected from you private. To protect the confidentiality of the data, responses will be transmitted in an encrypted format (readable only to the research team). Prolific participant IDs will only be collected for purposes of distributing payments and will not be included with your study responses or the study’s data set. The de-identified data may be shared publicly upon publication as required by journals’ in-house policies. The identifying information will not be shared.

In case you experience any technical difficulties and are unable to complete the task, please use the internal messaging system of Prolific to contact the researchers and receive payment for the time spent on the experiment (at $8/hour).

Once you complete this study (or do it partially) you will not be able to participate in the same study or similar ones from the Collective Cognition Lab again.

Your participation in this study is completely voluntary. You are free not to participate or to withdraw at any time, for whatever reason. No matter what decision you make, there will be no penalty or loss of benefits to which you are otherwise entitled.

For more information or questions about this research you may contact the Collective Cognition Lab at omohan@ur.rochester.edu. Please contact the University of Rochester Research Subjects Review Board at 265 Crittenden Blvd., CU 420628, Rochester, NY 14642, Telephone (585) 276-0005 or (877) 449-4441 for the following reasons:

You wish to talk to someone other than the research staff about your rights as research subject;

To voice concerns about the research;

To provide input concerning the research process;

In the event the study staff could not be reached.

By clicking "Next" you consent to participating in this experiment.
