Instructions



In this study, you will be required to complete a simple decision-making task as a pair with a randomly assigned partner.

In each round, you will be shown two colored squares. Please select one of the squares by clicking on it. You and your partner will both be rewarded with 1 point if you choose the same color. The reward will be shown to you before you proceed to the next round.

Your aim is to maximize your reward. You will receive $15 for your participation with an extra $0.01 for each point you receive in the task.

There are a total of 300 rounds. You will have two short breaks. During these breaks, you may rest your eyes or stretch but do not switch tabs or leave the page. Doing so will invalidate your study participation.

The study will be followed by two brief questions (max. 50 words response) asking you about your experience.

The completion code will be shown on the last page, after the questions.

Click 'Next' to begin.
