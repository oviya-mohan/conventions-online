Instructions



In this study, you will be required to complete a simple decision-making task.

In each round, you will be shown two colored squares. Please select one of the squares by clicking on it. Depending on your and your partner’s choice, you will either both be rewarded with 10 points, or neither of you will be rewarded. The reward will be shown to you before you proceed to the next round.

Your aim is to maximize your reward.

There are a total of 63 rounds .

If you have any questions about the instructions please feel free to ask the experimenter for clarifications before starting the study.
